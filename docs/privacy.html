Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

SnapCTRL is an open-source iOS client for Snapcast multi-room audio. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

SnapCTRL operates entirely on your local network and does not transmit any information to external servers, analytics services, or third parties.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Server connection history - IP addresses and ports of Snapcast servers you've connected to, for auto-reconnect functionality
  • Saved servers - Manually added server configurations

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by deleting the app.

Network Access

SnapCTRL requires local network access to:

  • Discover Snapcast servers via Bonjour/mDNS
  • Connect to Snapcast servers for audio streaming
  • Communicate with servers via JSON-RPC for control functions

All network communication occurs only within your local network.

Third-Party Services

SnapCTRL does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

SnapCTRL does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
